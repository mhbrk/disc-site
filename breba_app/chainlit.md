# 🚀 Welcome to Breba! 🤖

## 🧠 Breba — Prompts Are Code

**Breba** is a next-generation personalization and web creation platform where **prompts become code**.  
Write instructions in plain English, and Breba’s intelligent agents turn them into **fully deployed web pages — in just minutes.**

No need to write HTML, CSS, or JavaScript. Simply describe what you want, and Breba does the rest — powered by **advanced large language models** and **real-time adaptive agents.**

Breba helps you **build and publish websites effortlessly**, making web creation **easy, fast, and accessible to everyone** — whether you’re showcasing your portfolio, writing a blog, or launching a product page.

---

## 📄 How It Works

1. **Open Breba**  
2. **Write your instructions**, for example:  
   > Create a personal portfolio site with a dark theme, three sections (about, projects, contact), and a footer with social media links.  
3. **Breba’s agents** interpret your request and generate a fully functional, live web page.  
4. **Preview and refine** — make inline edits, upload assets, or adjust your prompt.  
5. **Deploy your app** when you are ready — it goes live instantly.

## 🧭 Interface Overview

**Tabs on the left:**

1. 🏠 **Home** — Opens [https://breba.app/home](https://breba.app/home)  
2. 📦 **Products** — View your projects. Create new ones or continue previous products.  
   - Type your instructions in plain English.  
   - Upload images or files as needed.  
   - Breba will clarify your request and generate a live, interactive web page.  
3. 🛠️ **Open Builder** — View the **specification** for your product, automatically generated from your prompts.  
4. 🚀 **Deploy App** — When ready, deploy your website: enter a name and click **Deploy**.  
   - You can now view all previous deployments linked to the product.  
5. 🧩 **Templates** — (In development) Explore and apply ready-made templates to jump-start your project.

## ✨ Key Features

- 📜 **Prompts as Code**  
  Describe your page in plain English. Breba immediately turns your instructions into a live, functional website — no coding required.

- 🤖 **Intelligent Agents**  
  Breba’s intelligent agents interpret, clarify, and expand your instructions to build exactly what you want.

- 🛠️ **Open Builder View**  
  Review the complete specification of your project that Breba generates from your prompts. This view helps you understand the structure and make precise changes.

- ⚙️ **Faster Inline Editing**  
  Make edits directly on the page. Breba now applies small updates instantly, without reloading or regenerating the entire layout.

- 🔄 **Smarter Difference Updates**  
  Breba’s agents now process smaller, faster changes by using difference-based updates, making the entire workflow smoother and more efficient.

- 📁 **Multiple File Uploads**  
  Upload several files in sequence before generating a preview. You no longer need to wait between uploads.

- 🚀 **User-Linked Deployments**  
  Each deployment is now connected to your user account and product. You can view all deployments for a product directly from the Deploy section.

- 💾 **Session Management**  
  Breba remembers your work automatically. You can continue previous sessions or switch between multiple projects without losing progress.

- 🧩 **Templates (Work in Progress)**  
  Templates are now visible in the sidebar, with one initial template available. More templates will be added soon to help you start new projects faster.
